by Emsky

LeagueSmith is a simple Masteries and Runes calculator for planning strategies in League of Legends.

To use the Mastery calculator: Click the item to which you'd like to add points. Right-click it to remove points.

To use the Rune calculator: Drag a rune from your rune collection to an appropriate slot, or click the rune itself. Right-click a rune on the rune page to remove it and place it back to your rune pool.
